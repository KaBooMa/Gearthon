Editor Controls

Modes

View

  • Left Mouse - Rotate around model
  • Right Mouse - Pan around model
  • Scroll Wheel - Zoom in/out of model

Attachment

  • Left Click - Select attachment at cursor
  • Ctrl + Left Click - Add new attachment
  • Right Click - Remove attachment at cursor
  • Ctrl + Mouse Move - Move selected attachment to model at cursor
  • Escape - Unselect current attachment
